Executionist vs Abolitionist

Over the period of 22 years, covering 1991 through 2012, we see a trend globally where more countries are resolving to either abolish or not recognize execution as a penalty.

During the same period of time, those countries that still recognize execution, have steadily decreased to where the total number of countries performing executions are half what they were in 1995.
